Ambient Noise

Ambient Noise is the first context aware mobile music player. Welcome to the Future.

Sign Up for the Beta!

Intelligent

Ambient Noise uses your smartphone's sensors to determine the best music to fit your environment based on information such as weather, who you're with and places you've previously tagged with a certain mood.

Personal

Sick of impersonal "Expert Curated Playlists"? Ambient Noise knows what you like and plays songs you want to listen to.

Simple

No need to decipher giant word dumps of genres and musicians or navigate multiple sub menus to get the music started. Just click play!
