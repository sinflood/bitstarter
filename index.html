WHAT IS AMBIENT NOISE

Intelligent

Ambient Noise uses your smartphone's sensors to determine the best music to fit your environment based on information such as weather, who you're with and places you've previously tagged with a certain mood.

Personal

Ambient Noise knows what you like and plays songs you actually want to listen to. No Expert Curated Playlists!

Simple

Just click play! No need to decipher giant word dumps of genres and musicians or navigate multiple sub menus to get the music started.
